1. Trung Nguyên Coffee

Trung Nguyen coffee is most famous for its strong taste and aroma. It is the leader local brand in VietNam and a big exprorter - you can find Trung Nguyen coffee in over 70 countries in the world. There is a wide brand of products diverse in the mix of coffee beans and processing methods. Trung Nguyen coffee has three main types: instant coffee, roasted coffee and distintive (weasel) one.

2. Vinacafe

Focusing more on the mild side, Vina Cafe is among the best choices for instant blended coffee. This brand is most famous for its cream, hazelnutty flavor for those wwith a sweet tooth. Vina Cafe has been the coffee of our childhood and still most common among Vietnamese households. The most popular one is VinaCafe 3-in-1, Wake-up Cafe with Weasel Flavor.

3. Nestcafe

NESTCAFE - the famous woldwide brand, comes with a lot of choices for the discerning market of Viet Nam coffee connoisseurs. Among their products, instants ones are top of mind. There blended coffee comes different levels of strong tastes that can be no less delicious than their ground coffee counterparts. Their NESCAFE cans Mocha/Latte are a good choice when you want just a pack of ready-made goodness.
